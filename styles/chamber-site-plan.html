Freetown Chamber of Commerce Site Plan

Site Purpose

The Freetown Chamber of Commerce website will:

  • Provide resources and networking opportunities for local businesses.
  • Offer a directory for businesses to showcase their services.
  • The Freetown Chamber of Commerce acts as a regional economic catalyst, leadership convener and community champion. We promote business through member services, economic and community development, and public policy that balances economic prosperity" - carlsbad.org site description

Scenarios

  • How can I find local businesses and services?
  • What networking events are coming up?
  • How can I register my business with the Freetown Chamber?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: (#2c3e50 - Header, Footer)

Accent Color: (#e74c3c - Buttons, Highlights)

Typography

Font: Poppins (Used for headings and body text)
